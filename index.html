Product Rec

What is ProductRec?

Product Rec is an application that provides personalized product recommendations in the e-commerce domain for a specific user. The application suggests products to display on the user's home screen without any need for a search query. Additionally, it can also provide product recommendations and rankings based on a user's search query.

How does it work?

ProductRec uses recommendation algorithms to analyze a user's purchase history and behavior on the website to provide personalized product recommendations. The algorithms consider various factors such as the user's search history, product similarities, and the behavior of similar users to generate recommendations that are most likely to be of interest to the user.
